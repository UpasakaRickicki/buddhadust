Saɱyutta Nikaya
Nidāna Vagga

Dhātu Saɱyutta

Sutta 1

Dhātu [Dhātunānatta] Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[pts][wp][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ||
Ekaɱ samayaɱ Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Dhātunānattaɱ vo bhikkhave, desissāmi,||
taɱ suṇātha sādhukaɱ manasi karotha||
bhāsissāmīti.|| ||

Evaɱ bhante ti kho te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Katamañ ca bhikkhave, dhātunānattaɱ?|| ||

4. Cakkhudhātu rūpadhātu cakkhu-viññāṇadhātu,||
sota-dhātu saddadhātu sota-viññāṇadhātu,||
ghāṇadhātu gandhadhātu ghāṇaviññāṇadhātu,||
jivhādhātu rasadhātu jivhā-viññāṇadhātu,||
kāyadhātu phoṭṭhabbadhātu kāya-viññāṇadhātu,||
manodhātu dhammadhātu mano-viññāṇadhātu.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, dhātunānattanti.|| ||
