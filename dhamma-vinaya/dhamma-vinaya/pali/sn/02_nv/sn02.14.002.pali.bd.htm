Saɱyutta Nikaya
Nidāna Vagga

Dhātu Saɱyutta

Sutta 2

Samphassa [Phassanānatta] Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[pts][wp][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ||
Ekaɱ samayaɱ Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Dhātunānattaɱ bhikkhave paṭicca uppajjati phassanānattaɱ.|| ||

3. Katamañ ca bhikkhave, dhātunānattaɱ?|| ||

4. Cakkhudhātu||
sota-dhātu||
ghāṇadhātu||
jivhādhātu||
kāyadhātu||
manodhātu.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave dhātunānattaɱ.|| ||

5. Katamañ ca bhikkhave, dhātunānattaɱ paṭicca uppajjati phassanānattaɱ?|| ||

6. Cakkhudhātuɱ bhikkhave, paṭicca uppajjati cakkhu-samphasso,||
sota-dhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati sota-samphasso,||
ghāṇadhātuɱ [141] paṭicca uppajjati ghāṇa-samphasso,||
jivhādhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati jivhāsamphasso,||
kāyadhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati kāya-samphasso,||
manodhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati mano-samphasso.|| ||

7. Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, dhātunānattaɱ paṭicca uppajjati phassanānattanti.|| ||
